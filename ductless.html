No Ducts? No Problem.

Flexible Comfort for Any Space

Ductless mini-split systems are one of the most versatile and energy-efficient HVAC solutions available. They provide both heating and cooling from a single compact unit — with no ductwork required. Perfect for older Ottawa homes, home additions, garages, or any space that needs targeted climate control.

Single Zone

Single-Zone Mini-Split Installation

One outdoor unit paired with one indoor air handler — ideal for a single room, a home addition, a detached garage, or a space with no existing ductwork. Quick to install with minimal disruption to your home.

  • Heating & cooling in one unit
  • Works efficiently down to -25°C
  • Wall-mounted & floor-mounted options
  • WiFi-enabled smart controls available
  • Energy-efficient inverter technology

Multi-Zone

Multi-Zone Mini-Split Systems

One outdoor unit connected to multiple indoor heads — each controllable independently. Great for whole-home ductless solutions or when you want to condition several distinct areas from a single system.

  • 2, 3, or 4-zone configurations available
  • Independent temperature control per zone
  • Simultaneous heating & cooling in different rooms
  • Reduces number of outdoor units needed
