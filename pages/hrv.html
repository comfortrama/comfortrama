Why Every Ottawa Home Needs an HRV

Modern homes in Ottawa are built tight for energy efficiency — but that means stale, humid indoor air can build up quickly. A Heat Recovery Ventilator (HRV) continuously replaces stale air with fresh outdoor air while recovering up to 80% of the heat from outgoing air. The result: healthier indoor air without wasting energy.

Maintenance

HRV Cleaning & Annual Service

HRV systems require annual cleaning to maintain performance and prevent buildup in the core and filters. A neglected HRV loses efficiency and can spread dust and allergens through your home. We recommend servicing every fall.

  • Core removal & cleaning
  • Filter replacement
  • Drain pan & condensate line cleaning
  • Airflow balancing & control check
  • Full system performance test
