On-Demand Hot Water

Endless Hot Water. Lower Energy Bills.

Unlike traditional tank water heaters that keep a large reservoir hot around the clock, tankless units heat water only when you need it. The result: endless hot water on demand, 15–25% lower energy costs, and a unit that lasts 20+ years — nearly twice as long as a conventional tank.

Annual Service

Tankless Maintenance & Descaling

Ottawa's hard water causes mineral buildup inside tankless heat exchangers over time. Annual descaling (flushing with a descaling solution) is essential to maintain efficiency and prevent premature failure.

  • Full system flush & descaling
  • Filter & inlet screen cleaning
  • Venting & combustion air inspection
  • Performance & temperature verification
